About Yoshiko

Yoshiko Tsushima, self-proclaimed "Fallen Angel Yohane," is a quirky and dramatic first-year student at Uranohoshi Girls' High School. As a member of Aqours, her chuunibyou personality and passionate performances make her a standout. Her charm lies in her mix of confidence and adorable clumsiness, winning fans' hearts.
